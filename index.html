Resurrection tourny rules

Teams and star players from all published BB2020 Rules Supplements and the NAF 2024 Document will be allowed. Any new Supplements that are published by October 1 2024 will be available for use so long as they are in Tourplay.

Teams will resurrect at the end of each game. No SPPs, injuries or any roster changes carry over between games

The 4 minute time limit will be enforced. Each coach may use one timeout per half for an extra 4 min on those dramatic, big turns. There will be a chess clock on each table. If the timer runs out, finish the currently activated player's action and end your turn.

Games will have a maximum length of 2 hours 30 minutes. In the unlikely event that a game exceeds 2.5 hrs, time is called, the dice are down and the match is over. There will be no OT.

Players must agree on cocked die rules, the use of dice cups at the start of the match. NO dice sharing will be allowed.

If there are any disagreements or rules disputes, tournament directors will be standing by to assist.

Team building:

All NAF sanctioned teams are eligible.

  • Teams get 1.25 million gold to construct their roster.
  • Teams must start play with a minimum of 11 players.
  • Teams can purchase Rerolls, apothecaries (if allowed), coaches, cheerleaders or Dedicated fans.
  • Masters of Undeath rule is allowed for the match. Rosters will reset at the end of the match.
Star players
  • Star players can be purchased as part of a roster, but only after you have at least 11 roster players
  • Highlander Rule: In the case that opposing teams hire the same star, both teams will NOT be allowed to use the star player.
  • Megastars are available to only Tier 4-5 teams.
Inducements
  • Teams may also include bribes as part of the roster as per the standard inducement cost.
  • Riotous Rookies, Team Mascot, Heady Brew, Kari Cold Steel, Papa Skullbones, Galandril  Silverwater, Grot Shockwhisker, Mungo Spinecracker, Fink Da Fixer, are available at standard cost.
  • Halfling Master Chefs can only be purchased by Halfling Thimble Cup Teams.
  • Giant Mercenaries are allowed
  • No other inducements may be purchased (including , cards, extra apothecaries, mercenaries, etc).
